About us

Celebrate in SNAP is an event supplies company. Our goal is to have products that are user-friendly, eco-friendly and budget-friendly.


User-friendly: Our time saving party packs are designed with YOU in mind- Easy set up and clean up means more time for fun! Our gifts are simple yet practical - exclusively put together with thoughtful details that make them so unique and special.


Eco-friendly: We use minimal packaging that is mostly fabric, paper or plastic that can be reused, repurposed or recycled - why? Because it is important to be good to our planet.


Budget-friendly: Quality does not always have to be expensive - and more money in your pocket means more smiles all around!


So get ready to "Celebrate in a SNAP"! 3..2...1...Party!
